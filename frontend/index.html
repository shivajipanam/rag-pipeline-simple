How it works

From chat export to answers in seconds

No setup, no integrations, no data leaving your session. Just upload and ask.

1
Upload your export
Drop your WhatsApp .zip or .txt export, a PDF transcript, or any text file. Volo AI accepts them all in one click.
2
We index and embed it
Messages are parsed, chunked into semantic windows, and embedded into a private vector store. Your data stays in your session — not shared, not stored permanently.
3
Ask anything
Query in plain English. Get instant answers powered by Groq Llama 3.1 with cited source messages. Export structured data to Excel in one click.
